About the Project

TerraGuard is a state-of-the-art landslide prediction system designed to mitigate natural disasters through data science. By leveraging historical geological records and real-time environmental data, our model identifies high-risk zones with high precision.

Our ensemble learning approach combines the strengths of various algorithms, ensuring robust predictions even in diverse terrain conditions.
